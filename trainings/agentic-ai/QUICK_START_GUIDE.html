What is Agentic AI? (5 minutes)

Simple Definition

An agentic AI system is an AI that can:

1. Understand what goal you want to achieve

2. Plan how to break that goal into steps

3. Take actions (using tools, calling APIs, running code)

4. Evaluate if it achieved the goal

5. Adjust its approach if needed

Core Concepts Explained Simply

1. Autonomy Levels

Level 1: AI makes suggestions only
Level 2: AI takes actions with human approval each time
Level 3: AI takes actions independently but reports them
Level 4: AI takes actions independently without reporting

Choose based on:

  • How safe are the actions?
  • How urgent is the task?
  • How much can you trust the results?
